Term: “Informal Tone”

Deﬁnition:
An informal tone, also called a casual tone, is appropriate for talking to a friend and usually uses ﬁrst person.
See http://www.word-mart.com/html/formal_and_informal_writing.html for a comparison between formal
and informal writing. An informal tone is usually avoided in essay writing.
