Term: “Signiﬁcant Misunderstanding”

Deﬁnition:
Ideas that include signiﬁcant misunderstandings reveal serious content gaps that impede the scholar from
fully addressing the task and potentially seriously confuse the reader. These misunderstandings are blatant
and critical for a scholar to address the task at hand.
