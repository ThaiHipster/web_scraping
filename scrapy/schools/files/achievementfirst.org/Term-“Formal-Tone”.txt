Term: “Formal Tone”

Deﬁnition:
Formal tone is an appropriate tone for an essay or other formal piece of writing. In an essay, third person is
almost always used. See http://www.word-mart.com/html/formal_and_informal_writing.html for a
comparison between formal and informal writing
