Term: “Basic Literal Understanding”

Deﬁnition:
The scholar demonstrates that they have a literal standing of the text/content and task. There are no major
misconceptions, but there might be a few minor inaccuracies. While this might include basic inferences, these
ideas primarily live in the literal.
