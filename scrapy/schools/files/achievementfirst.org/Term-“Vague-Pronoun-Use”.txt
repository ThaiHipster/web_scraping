Term: “Vague Pronoun Use”

Deﬁnition:
When there are vague pronouns references, it is unclear which noun a pronoun refers to. Every pronoun
should have one and only one clear noun antecedent. (See http://wwwnew.towson.edu/ows/proref.htm for
examples)
