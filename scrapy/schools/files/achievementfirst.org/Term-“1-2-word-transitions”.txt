Term: “1-2 word transitions”

Deﬁnition:
One to two word transitions are used to link one idea to the next. Scholars may attempt to use transitions
words that are not the most appropriate or the best choice. Scholars may also use the same transition
repeatedly. A list of transitions can be found at http://www.smart-words.org/transition-words.html
