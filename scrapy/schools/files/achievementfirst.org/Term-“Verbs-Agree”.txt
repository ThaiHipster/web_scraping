Term: “Verbs Agree”

Deﬁnition:
Subject and verbs must be in agreement. The child sings. The children sing. Errors occur more frequently
when the subject and verb are separated by phrases or clauses. (See
http://leo.stcloudstate.edu/grammar/subverag.html for more examples)
