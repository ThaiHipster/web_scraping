Term: “Topic Sentence”

Deﬁnition:
The topic sentence articulates what the paragraph is about although it is not a sub-argument of the thesis. This
is frequently seen when a response is just one paragraph, or if a response is two to three separate paragraphs
without a thesis or controlling idea.
