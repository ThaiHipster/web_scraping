Leave Your Mark at Somerset Academy!
Buy a Commemorative Brick!
Special Price:  $50
The Somerset Academy PTSO is joining with the leaders of our school to raise funds by providing a program, where lasting memories can be celebrated. We are implementing the Bricks ’R US program which enables us to purchase commemorative bricks to be displayed in the school courtyard adjacent to the gymnasium. The commemorative bricks are great gifts to give to your graduating seniors. These bricks can also be used to honor your child or loved ones, celebrate a teacher, or promote a school club or achievement.  
These bricks are available for purchase in two different sizes. 
The 4x8 inches brick cost $50 and provides 3 lines with up to 21 characters in each line.
The 8x8 inches brick cost $100 and provides 6 lines with up to 21 characters line. 
You may purchase online using PayPal for payment and complete the order form below and return to the Activities Office. If you decide to pay with a check make checks payable to Somerset Academy PTSO and attach to the below order form and return to the Activities Office. 
If you have any questions please contact the PTSO at 
ptso@somersetacademy.com
 . 
Thank you for your support,
Somerset Academy PTSO
