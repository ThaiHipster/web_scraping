Public Registration Lottery Selection Rules and     Commitments
Re-registration  forms sent home to students:  February 6th
Sibling  registration forms available from the school office:   February 6th 
Re-registration  of current students and registration of siblings:   February 6th  - 17th
Public  Registration begins:  February 27th 
Public  Registration ends:  March 23rd 
P
osting of lottery recipients on the internet:   April 5
th
 at 3pm
Deadline  for receipt of acceptance letter from lottery recipients:  April 7th MIDNIGHT
Deadline  to submit completed registration packets and all required materials to complete  enrollment process  April 28th
Selected lottery recipients will be posted by incoming grade and      registration number at the school location where you registered and on the     schools  web-site, 
www.somersetacademy.com  
 after 3:00 p.m. on  April 5th,
 2017.  It is the parent's responsibility to determine if  your child was selected  to  
 attend the Somerset Academy  Charter campus for  which you registered  through the  
lottery  process.  The parent further acknowledges that due to the large  number of   
 sibling registrants, all siblings may not be accepted.    
Only one registration application per child will be accepted or     permitted  per campus location.
Should your child be selected to attend Somerset Academy Charter   through   the  lottery process, you must notify  the Somerset Academy Charter Campus at which your child was   selected in 
  writing
 to either accept or decline the selection by midnight on April 7th.
Failure to notify   Somerset Academy   Charter in writing   of acceptance, shall constitute   that you decline the  selection  and Somerset  Academy  Charter will   contact the next student on the  wait  list.
Once you have accepted the lottery selection in writing, the School Registrar will email you a Registration Packet which you must complete and submit by April 28, 2017.
