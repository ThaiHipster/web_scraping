Somerset Achievements
Visual Arts Conservatory Students Create Portraits for Filipino Children!
																															In October and November, students in the Visual Arts, Arts Conservatory program created portraits for a group of Filipino children for the Memory Project.  
The Memory Project is a charitable nonprofit organization that invites art teachers and their students to create and donate portraits to youth around the world who have faced substantial challenges, such as neglect, abuse, loss of parents, violence, and extreme poverty.
NAHS Raises Homeless Awareness!
																															In September and October, National Art Honor Society participated in Miami-Dade Homeless Trust Roofs Rising Project.  Roofs Rising is an art installation intended to teach our community about the need for housing.
Panther Marching Band:  Runner-Up at States!
																															Congratulations to the Panther Marching Band who earned 2nd Place at the FMBC State Marching Band Competition in Tampa for their show "Ghost Town."  Special thank you to Ms. Ortega, Ms. Crespo, all the instructors and band parents for their outstanding dedication to the band program.
