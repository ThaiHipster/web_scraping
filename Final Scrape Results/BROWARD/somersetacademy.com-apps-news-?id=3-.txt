Visual Arts Conservatory Students Create Portraits for Filipino Children!
													In October and November, students in the Visual Arts, Arts Conservatory program created portraits for a group of Filipino children for the Memory Project.  
The Memory Project is a charitable nonprofit organization that invites art teachers and their students to create and donate portraits to youth around the world who have faced substantial challenges, such as neglect, abuse, loss of parents, violence, and extreme poverty.
NAHS Raises Homeless Awareness!
													In September and October, National Art Honor Society participated in Miami-Dade Homeless Trust Roofs Rising Project.  Roofs Rising is an art installation intended to teach our community about the need for housing.
Panther Marching Band:  Runner-Up at States!
													Congratulations to the Panther Marching Band who earned 2nd Place at the FMBC State Marching Band Competition in Tampa for their show "Ghost Town."  Special thank you to Ms. Ortega, Ms. Crespo, all the instructors and band parents for their outstanding dedication to the band program.
Panther for Life!
													Congrats to baseball player, Juan J. Teixeira, who signed with Florida International University on NCAA Signing Day.
Congrats Varsity Swimmers!
													Congratulations to Varsity Swimmers (Isabella Di Salvo, Elise Larin,  Kyana Castro, Katrina Del Vecchio) for having a great meet at the FHSAA Swimming State Finals!  Click here for results.
Congratulations to Artist, Adiela Pena! 
													Adiela's artwork was selected through jury process to be displayed in the Broward County High School Art All-Stars Exhibition at Broward College.  It will be on display from November 2-8th.
Congrats Miami Dolphins Player of the Week!
													Congratulations to Varsity Football player, Ryle Aguila, for being selected as the Miami Dolphins High School Player of the Week presented by retired Dolphin Player, Troy Dayton.  Ryle will be recognized during half-time at the Dolphin vs Raiders game!
National Champion Wrestler, Nick Benton!
													On November 21st,  in Cedar Falls, Iowa, Nick Benton became a National Champion and All-American at the USA Wrestling Preseason National Championship at 182 pounds. 
Teammate Daniel Miranda also placed 5th at 195 pounds.  Go Panthers!
Girls Swim Team Members Going to States!
													Congratulations to the Varsity Girls Swim Team members who qualified for the State Championships!
