					Pay School Lunch Accounts Online
Check-out our new online lunch payment option.  
Go to the Somerset Main Webpage and click on "Online Payments"
Then find your school and make a credit card payment...it's that easy!
Students that are 
not
 up-to-date with cafeteria payments will receive the following:
BREAKFAST:
a cereal box and a milk carton
 instead of the daily breakfast selection
LUNCH:  a cheese sandwich and a milk carton instead of the daily lunch selection
Payments can made either by cash, check or online payments.
Checks can be made payable to:  Somerset Academy
                    20803 Johnson Street
                    Pembroke Pines, FL 33029
                   (954) 442-0233
