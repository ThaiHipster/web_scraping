												School Profile									
Our Mission
Somerset Academy School promotes a culture that maximizes student achievement  and fosters the development of responsible, self-directed life-long learners in  a safe and enriching environment.
Our Vision
Somerset Academy School is dedicated to providing equitable high quality  education for all students.
Accreditation
Our High school and Arts Conservatory Accreditation is by SACS Southern  Association of Colleges and Schools.
Curriculum
Students in Grades 9-12 follow a college preparatory  curriculum and are required to meet all the requirements for a standard Florida  diploma. 
Advanced Placement
Advanced Placement courses are offered through the College Board Advanced  Placement Program in Art History, Biology, Calculus AB, Chemistry, English  Language, English Literature, European History, Psychology, Physics B, Physics  C, Spanish Language, Spanish Literature, Statistics, United States Government  & Politics, United States History, and World History.
All students enrolled in AP courses are required to take AP exams.
