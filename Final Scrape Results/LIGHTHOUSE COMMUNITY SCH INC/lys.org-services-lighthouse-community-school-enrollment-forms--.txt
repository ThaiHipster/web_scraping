Enrollment Forms
Lighthouse Community School is open to students ages 11-22 in grades 6-12 who meet at least one of the following criteria.
are experiencing family conflict(runaways);
are placed in out-of-home care by Departments of Job and Family Services or other governmental agencies;
are involved with the juvenile justice system or other public system;
are a Lighthouse Youth Services client (residential treatment, group home care, foster care, independent living, or family outreach);
have been expelled or suspended from school more than once in the past year;
are currently on IEP’s and have a disability determination of emotional disturbance;
who are at least one grade level behind their cohort age groups;
experience crises that significantly interfere with academic progress in a traditional school setting.
