Graduation Requirements
The Lighthouse Community School is a diploma granting school.
Requirements include:
 22 units of credit distributed per the following list, for successful completion of the goals and objectives of his/her individual education plan (IEP)
English                                       4 credits
Social Studies                           3 credits (1 U.S. History, 1/2 Government, 1/2 Economics)
Mathematics                             3 credits
Science                                       3 credits (1 Biology, 1 Physical Science)
Physical Education/Health     1 credit
Business/Technology              1 credit
Electives                                     7 credits
Total                                             22 credits
Passed all five parts of the Ohio Graduation Test unless specifically exempted from the test by the IEP
A completed approved resume on file in the student’s record and graduation portfolio
20 hours of community service
A writing sample that addresses and describes the student’s career and personal goals
Completion of the student’s Individualized Education Plan (IEP), goals and transition planning objectives
Visit  
Ohio Means Jobs 
 for information about employment and education options after receiving a high school diploma.
