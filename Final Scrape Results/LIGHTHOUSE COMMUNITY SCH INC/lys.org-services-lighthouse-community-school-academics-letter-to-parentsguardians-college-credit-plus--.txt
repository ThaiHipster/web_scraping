Letter to Parents/Guardians: College Credit Plus
Ohio’s College Credit Plus program offers students in grades 7-12 the chance to earn college credit from any Ohio public college or participating private college while earning high school credit for the same course, if they qualify academically.
Deadline to apply is April 1, 2016.
