Welcome to Simon Tech Students & Parents, 
 The 2017-2018 school year promises to be a great one as we will be graduating our fifth senior class. 
Who's is your counselor? 
Ms. Polonco (9th & 11th Grade)﻿
Ms. Habrun (10th & 12th Grade)
Ms. Hernandez - Student Transition Coordinator & Alumni Tracker 
 We are so excited to have all of you as a part of our school, and we look
 forward to getting to know you more this year. We will be using Naviance again with you this year. Naviance is a Web-based tool to help students navigate the career and college process. With Naviance you will be able to:
Research Careers & Colleges
Search for scholarships to help fund your post-secondary educational and enrichment opportunities over the summer
Create & update your resume
Send questions to your counselor or schedule an appointment
Manage your college application process
Request transcripts 
Complete interest inventories
Log into Naviance for links to Collegeboard, FAFSA (Financial Aid), CSU Mentor, UC Doorways, SAT, ACT, links to scholarships,     and much more..
As your College Counselors:
Our role is to support the academic achievement of all students
Our overall focus is to assist you in your academic, personal, and career goals
 We hope to make your transition into adulthood a smooth one and for all students to become productive members of society
Simon Tech is a college preparatory high school so make sure to use Naviance in your college and career exploration process during advisory and at home
If you have any questions or concerns remember our conversations are confidential and our doors are always open.
