School Coordinating Council
SCHOOL COORDINATING COUNCIL
Purpose
: 
To provide suggestions and recommendations to the principal on day-to-day operations and issues including, but not limited to budgeting, curriculum, school policies, school/community participation and the general direction of the school.
The Council must also help develop and approve the School Safety Plan and Local Control Accountability Plan (LCAP) each year.
