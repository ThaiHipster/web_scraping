HOW TO ADD CREDIT TO YOUR CHILD'S LUNCH ACCOUNT
Option 1
:  Visit Cafeteria Manager during the school day and pay cash at the window.
Option 2
:  Online -
1) 
Visit My Payments Plus
 and create an account in upper left hand side. You will need your child's 10-digit ID.
2) Once signed in, you will see a highlighted yellow box under the column, "Payment".  Enter your desired amount and it will take you through secure steps on entering your credit card information.
