ABOUT
Our before-school and after-school programs encourage families to play an active role in the quality experiences that the Y provides for your children.  They provide a fun, safe place for your children as well as opportunities to explore their interests through our enrichment programs. Financial assistance is available to qualifying families based on funds.
Benefits include year-round care, 1:12 staff to child ratio and enrichment instructors with a background in education and child development. Curriculum includes homework time, fun enrichment classes, activities throughout the day and the chance to work with community organizations.
Because our programs is located on-site at Van Gogh you don’t have to worry about transportation and we provide care on all minimum school days for no additional cost.
Our child care staff is CPR/first aid and water safety certified and fingerprinted to ensure quality and safety for your child. Y staff has also completed training and coursework in child development and early childhood education.
* On-site at Van Gogh Charter School
* Before-school care starting at 6:30 am and after-school care 
until 6:30 pm
* Homework time/educational support
* Educational, recreation & fine arts enrichment
* Family & Community Involvement
* Financial Assistance Available
The Y: We're for youth development, healthy living, and social responsibility.
