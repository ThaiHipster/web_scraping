School Board of Directors
The Simon Tech Board of Directors meets quarterly and is appointed by the Alliance for College-Ready Public Schools. The School Board is responsible for all aspects of the day-to-day operations, including but not limited to, making necessary provisions for accounting, budgeting, payroll, purchasing, liability, insurance, and the like.
