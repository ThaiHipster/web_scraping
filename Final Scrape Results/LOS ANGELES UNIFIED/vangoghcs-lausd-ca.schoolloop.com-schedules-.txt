OFFICE HOURS
The school office is open Monday-Friday from 7:30am to 4:30pm.  There is an answering machine to leave messages when the office is closed.
REGULAR SCHOOL DAYS
Drop-off: 
Students may begin arriving at 7:45AM, PLEASE DO NOT DROP THEM OFF BEFORE THIS TIME, THERE IS NO SUPERVISION ON THE SCHOOLYARD. Only students under YMCA care are allowed onto campus before this time.
If you do drive your child to school, please park across the street from the school and cross AT THE CROSSWALK.  We have an excellent curbside valet program on the Titian Street & Van Gogh Charter School entrances if you would like to simply drop your child off & continue your morning drive. To learn more, or volunteer to help on the valet lines, 
click here
.

BANKED SCHOOL DAYS
Tuesdays are always shorter days to allow staff to attend professional development meetings. The hours are “banked” onto other days so that your children receive all their instructional time.
Dismissal:
 All Tuesday dismissals will be 1:20PM. Please be on time!
