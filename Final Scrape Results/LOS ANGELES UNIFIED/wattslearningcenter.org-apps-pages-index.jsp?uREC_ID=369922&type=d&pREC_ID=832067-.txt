WATTS LEARNING CENTER
COMMUNITY COLLABORATORIUM
RETHINKING EDUCATION – QUALITY OF LIFE – PROBLEM SOLVING
We are engaged in re-thinking the purpose of education.  We are interested in solving problems to improve the quality of life locally and internationally.
The WLC hosts a monthly Community Collaboratorium, bringing together key constituents from the surrounding community and concerned citizens to identify strategies and opportunities improve the quality of life in the community.  We are interested in increasing the opportunities for our schools and children to engage in deeper learning experiences in the community. 
Goal:
Develop and implement strategies for the long-term benefit of children, families and the community.
Outcomes: 
Develop common goals 
Determine how our schools can work and learn in the community 
Develop a plan of action
