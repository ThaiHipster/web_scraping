Eligibility Criteria
All applicants for 6th grade must be:
DC residents
Entering the 6th grade for the 2018-2019 academic year.
Born on or after February 1, 2006
All applicants for 7th grade must be:
DC residents
Entering the 7th grade for the 2018-2019 academic year.
Born on or after February 1, 2005
All applicants for 8th grade must be:
DC residents
Entering the 8th grade for the 2018-2019 academic year.
Born on or after February 1, 2004
Waiting list
SEED is currently accepting application for our 6th, 7th, and 8th grade waiting lists.  You may apply on 
My School DC
.  Families will be notified as space becomes available.  Once accepted off of the waiting list, families have 48 hours to accept their offer and submit enrollment documents to SEED DC to secure their child’s seat for the 2017-2018 school year.
