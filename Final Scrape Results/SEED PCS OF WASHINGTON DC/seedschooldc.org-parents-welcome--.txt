Welcome to the Office of Family & Community Engagement! We look forward to partnering with you on your child's journey through college. Please check back on this page frequently for updated key events, key forms, and more! 
The mission of the Office of Family & Community Engagement is to:
Build trusting relationships between the staff, families, and the community
Build a learning network: Connect families to student learning goals and provide learning opportunities to support parents as teachers; Create a Dorm and Academic network that supports families through effective programming
Coach staff and families in developing stronger relationships
Collaborate with community partners and support the needs of the school community.
For more information, please contact Mrs. Rashida Holman-Jones at (202)-248-7773 x5050 or at 
rholman@seedschooldc.org
.
