Ways to Give
The SEED School of Washington, D.C. welcomes contributions of any size to support SEED DC's mission and students. If you are not sure about how you would like to give or what areas you would like to support, our Development Staff is available to discuss the school’s vision and options for giving.
One of the simplest ways to support SEED DC is with a financial contribution--which can be donated via cash, a check, or a credit card. The SEED School of Washington, D.C. is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization, which means your donation is tax-deductible to the full extent provided by law.
