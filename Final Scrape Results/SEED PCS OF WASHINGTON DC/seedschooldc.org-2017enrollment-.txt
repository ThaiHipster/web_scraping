welcome to the seed family!
Results from the My School DC lottery will be shared on March 31, 2017. Please login to your My School DC Family Portal for the status of your application and to verify where your child(ren) is matched for the 2017-2018 school year.
New and returning families: please see below for key information and requirements as you prepare to re-enroll your child(ren) at SEED DC for the 2017-2018 school year. 
new seed dc families
Congratulations and welcome! All newly accepted SEED DC families must complete all of the required enrollment documentation by May 1, 2017.
InfoSnap Online Registration – Parents will receive snap codes via email by April 3rd. Please contact us at admissions@seedschooldc.org if you need assistance with this process.  To access InfoSnap, click
