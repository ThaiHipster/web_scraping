Parent Connect
Parent Connect
 (txConnect) allows parents and students to view 
grades, 
assignments (grades 2-12), 
STAAR (and TAKS) results, 
immunizations, 
attendance and discipline information (grades EE-12) online.
Students perform better in school when their parents are actively involved in their learning. Use parent Connect to keep you informed of your child’s performance!
Get automatic email alerts of unexcused absences and failing averages or assignment grades.
See results and information on STAAR (TAKS)
View information on attendance
See detailed grades on homework, classwork, tests, progress reports and report cards
Use the searchable assignments page to stay up-to-date on required coursework
Customize alert notifications for grades and attendance
Get Started Here
New to Parent Connect?
You will need to complete a two-step process to create a new account by following these simple instructions:
Step One:
On the Parent Connect link on the home page, click on “New txConnect User”
You will need your child(ren)’s student portal ID Number in order to complete the process, which you can get from your campus registrar.
Step Two:
On the summary page click on the blue “settings” button.
Click on the “register” button. If you receive an error message regarding your email address, please contact your campus registrar.
Start here to register or to log-in
Need help?
Contact you’re the Registrar at your Southwest Schools campus today.
