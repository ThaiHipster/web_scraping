How to Donate
Financial Support
We are pleased that you are interested in making a difference in the lives of the students at Southwest Schools.
To donate, use our 
secure, online donations site 
 or mail checks to:
Southwest Schools
Attn: Donations
3333 Bering Drive
Houston, Texas  77057
*If donating by check, please include a completed “
Tax Deductible Donations Form
”
so we may direct your funds appropriately.
Gift Matching
Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match any charitable contributions or volunteer hours made by their employees. If your company is eligible, request a matching gift form from your employer, and send it completed and signed with your gift. We will do the rest. The impact of your gift to Southwest Schools may be doubled!
Volunteer
There are countless opportunities to make a difference through volunteering.  Contact any of our campus to find out how you can help.
Other Opportunities
There are lots of ways to get involved and make a direct impact on the lives of the students at Southwest.  Contact Marcus Brewer, Director of Community Relations and Partnerships at 713-784-6345 or email 
mbrewer@swschools.org
