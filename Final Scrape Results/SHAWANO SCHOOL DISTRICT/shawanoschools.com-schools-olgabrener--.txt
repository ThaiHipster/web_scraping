Olga Brener Fast Facts
Start time is 8:20 a.m. Doors lock at 8:19 a.m. Dismissal is at 3:20 p.m.
Computer lab and the library are open every morning at 7:05 a.m.
Breakfast starts at 7:30 a.m. and runs until 8:10 a.m.
After school lab is open until 3:55 p.m. on Monday-Thursday. There is no
 lab on Fridays or the last school day of the week.  
The Brener phone number is (715) 524-2131. 
Office extensions are 8401 or 8402. 
The attendance line can be reached by hitting the # key. 
If your child is absent, please call before 9:00 am if possible.
Lunch/recess milk money may be added to your child’s account at any time. 
Deposits may be turned into the office or to the classroom teacher. Envelopes are available in the office. 
Payments may also be made online through Skyward.  Please note that recess milk is optional and is not part of the free or reduced program and must be paid for a week at a time. Balances are checked on the last school day of the week for the next week.
We have an active PTO that would love your help! Please contact a PTO member or the office to see how you can join or help.
We hope you enjoy your time as a Brener Hawk –

earning, Living, Leading the Hawk Way! 
Mission Statement 
Olga Brener Intermediate School, in cooperation with its families and community, will prepare students academically and socially to their fullest potential; therefore, becoming life-long learners and responsible citizens.
