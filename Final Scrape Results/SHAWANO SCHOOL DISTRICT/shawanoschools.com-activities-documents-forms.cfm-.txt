Attention Parents and Student-Athletes
The 2017-18 school year forms and eligibility video will be available on-line starting Wed. July 12th. Each student-athlete and parent/guardian are required to watch the eligibility video and sign all required forms in order to participate in Shawano Athletics for the 2017-18 school year.
Each program will host Parent meetings to address sport specific policies and expectations. All required forms must be on file and verified in the High School office before beginning practice! 
New for the 2017-18 school  year.
Concussion Insurance
The WIAA will be providing concussion insurance to its member schools beginning August 1, 2017. Student athletes who are injured in practice or play are covered.
1. The insurance will pay for covered charges after the primary insurance has been exhausted.
2. Also, referred to as “secondary policy” – in that it will pay secondary to any primary insurance in place.
3. The insurance will also pay for any covered charges the primary insurance will not cover (including deductibles, co-pays, any other out-of-pocket charges).
