Board of Education
School Board meetings are held the first and third Mondays of the month at 5:30 P.M. in the Library at the Shawano Community High School.
School Board Committees are scheduled to meet at 5:30 p.m. on the following days of the month.
Executive Board - first Wednesday of the month *
Buildings & Grounds - third Wednesday of the month *
Finance - second Wednesday of the month *
Policy/Curriculum - second Monday of the month *
* Meeting times and days are subject to change
