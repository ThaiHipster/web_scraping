					Detroit Enterprise Academy Offers Parents Free Smartphone App				
Press Release				
					1/20/2014				
Page Content
DETROIT, Mich., Jan. 20, 2014
—Detroit Enterprise Academy isproviding a free smartphone app to keep parents connected to the school at alltimes. 
The appprovides instant access to student grades, school news, and meal balanceaccounts. Parents can also email their child’s teacher or search the school’sonline carpooling database.
“Maintainingstrong communication with our parents is a priority at Detroit Enterprise Academy,so we are excited to offer this new tool to channel information even faster,”said Principal Chanavia Patterson.​
Parents candownload the National Heritage Academies (NHA) app for free and use theirnormal log in information to be connected to their child’s school. 
Detroit Enterprise  Academy is a free public charter school, managed by NHA, that servesstudents in kindergarten through eighth grade.
