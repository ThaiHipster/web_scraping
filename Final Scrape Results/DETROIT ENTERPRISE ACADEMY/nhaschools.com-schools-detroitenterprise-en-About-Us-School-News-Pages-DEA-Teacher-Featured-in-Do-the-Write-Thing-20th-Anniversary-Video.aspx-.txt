					Detroit Enterprise Academy Dean Featured in Do the Write Thing 20th Anniversary Video				
Do the Write Thing				
					10/5/2015				
Page Content
Detroit Enterprise Academy Dean Zemen Maruggi is featured in a new video highlighting the 20 year anniversary of Do the Write Thing, a national writing contest addressing youth violence.  DEA is proud to serve as a host school for this program. You can watch the video highlighting 20 years here: 
http://youtu.be/ou5ESn0QB0k
.
Each year, one male and one female student from schools across Detroit are selected as finalists. Each year, one male and one female student from schools across Detroit are selected as finalists. A DEA studnt has won the last two years and both students enjoyed a free trip to Washington, D.C. where they met lawmakers, visited historical sites, and now have their writing entered in the Library of congress.
