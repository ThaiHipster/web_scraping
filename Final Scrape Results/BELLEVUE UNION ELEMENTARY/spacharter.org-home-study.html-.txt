What is Home Study?
A free public school (K-12) educational alternative
An at-home teaching environment
School District support including a credentialed teacher, curriculum, materials and other resources
Parents providing day-to-day support for their child based on a written agreement
Advantages of Home Study
Provides one-to-one support for students
Curriculum is individualized
Students may progress at their own pace
Accommodates various learning styles
Greater flexibility of school time
The community becomes the classroom
Educational continuity can be maintained while traveling
How Does it Work?
Parent submits an application and meets with the teacher.
An independent study agreement is signed, an orientation is held with books, materials, and assignments distributed.
Meetings are held regularly with teacher to provide support.
At the end of the agreement period a progress report is completed.
What is provided?
Credentialed teacher for support and supervision
Textbooks, workbooks, teachers' editions
Health screenings and services as needed
