Alex Jimenez
Counselor
Mr. Jimenez was raised and currently lives in Petaluma, where he attended Santa Rosa Junior College before transferring to and graduating from University of California, Santa Cruz with a BA in Sociology. He is currently finishing his MA in Counselor Education at San Jose State University. This is Mr. Jimenez’s third year at Stony Point Academy, first as an Americorp member and then as the academic adviser/school counselor. He is excited to see SPA grow and help support their students’ goals and dreams!
Hello
Welcome new and returning Stony Point Academy students! My name is Alexis Jimenez and I am your school counselor; this will be my third year working at SPA. I am excited to get to know you and support your goals and dreams! ​
​
In my own time, I like to hike, camp, go to concerts, and spend time with family and friends. Please feel free to stop by to say hi, or ask any question. Let’s make this a great school year!
