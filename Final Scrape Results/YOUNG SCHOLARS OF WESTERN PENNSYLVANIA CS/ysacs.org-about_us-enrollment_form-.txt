Young Scholar's Academy Enrollment Form
Please click one of the following formats to download and print our enrollment form:   
PDF
 Young Scholar's Academy is a year-round public, charter school and there is no tuition to Arizona residents.  In order for your child to attend, they must first be placed on our waiting list. Please complete an enrollment form and submit it to our office during business hours or by fax. Eligibility is determined by a first-come, first-serve basis and we abide by the date the completed form is received in our office.
The same form may be used to place your child on our waiting list for
Young Scholar's Academy Pre-School.
