Governing Board Meetings
Young Scholar's Academy will hold a Governing Board meeting on the first Wednesday of each month, at 5:00 p.m., at 1501 E. Valencia Road, Fort Mohave, Arizona, which is open to the public. If any special accommodation is required, please contact the school office at least 24 hours prior to the meeting. The Governing Board may vote to go into executive session, which will not be open to the public, for legal advice or to discuss records exempt by law from public inspection.
