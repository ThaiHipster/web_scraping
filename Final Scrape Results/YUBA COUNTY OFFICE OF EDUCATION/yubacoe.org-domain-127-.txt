Independent Study
Welcome to the Independent Studies program at Yuba County Charter. Independent Studies is a voluntary program choice made by families for a myriad of reasons. This link to the California Department of Education (CDE) discusses some of the main reasons why families may choose Independent Studies, as well as providing advice to parents working with students at home: 
http://www.cde.ca.gov/sp/eo/is/ 
For our Program, your options are endless. While we offer students who are able to meet the academic standard a weekly meeting with their academic coach and teacher, there are far more options available. There is a computer lab, Library, and Study Hall where para-educators and tutors work one on one and in small groups with students from 8am-4pm daily. Also if there is an individual subject you struggle with, we have classes available daily that you can register into to receive a more traditionally paced curriculum in that area, while remaining on IS.
Our focus extends beyond just strong academics, but includes these additional options to aid in the need to have active social and community lives as citizens. Let us help you, help yourself to reach your fullest potential.
