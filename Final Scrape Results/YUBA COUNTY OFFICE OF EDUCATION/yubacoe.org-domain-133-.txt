School Hours
8:00am to  5:00pm, Monday through Friday.  Please check our calendar for events.
Directions
The Charter School is located in the middle of Marysville, close to Ellis Lake and the downtown area.
From Yuba City on Highway 20 East, turn left on F Street and then an immediate right on 11th Street.  Turn left on E Street and the Charter School is the building to your left.
From Sacramento on Highway 70 North, continue in the right hand lane and do not swing West on Highway 20, but go straight as it becomes E Street.  After a 1/8 of a mile, the Charter School is on your left.
From Oroville/Chico on Highway 70 South, exit right onto 14th Street and continue West.  At the stop light, turn left onto E Street and continue North.  The Charter School is on your right.
