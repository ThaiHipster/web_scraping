May 31, 2014
Champion X2!
Another successful day in Olean at the Section VI Track & Field Championships as Senior Dawan Jones captures the title in the Triple Jump.  Also, the Girls 4x100 Relay finished 3rd overall in Division II with a school-record time of 51.37.  Great day for the ThunderHawks!! Off to Syracuse next weekend for the NY State Championships.

SECTION VI Track & Field Champion!
Senior Dawan Jones won the Section VI Championship in the Long Jump Friday afternoon in Olean, while Senior Juan Martinez placed 5th in the Long Jump.  There'll be more competition tomorrow as the 2014 Section VI Track & Field Championships continue.
