The Sarah Thorp Acton Scholarship Fund
In honor of our beloved teacher Sarah Thorp Acton (April 12, 1981-March 25, 2013) a scholarship fund has been set up by her family and Tapestry Charter School as of March 2013. For more information or to donate please contact the Development Office at 716-204-5883 or make your donation directed to the Sarah Thorp Acton Scholarship Fund. Checks may be addressed to the fund and sent to Tapestry Charter School, 65 Great Arrow Avenue, Buffalo, NY 14216. Thank you.
