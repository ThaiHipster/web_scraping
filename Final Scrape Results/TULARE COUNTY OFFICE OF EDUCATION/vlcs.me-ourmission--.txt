Serving students on site in grades K - 8 and Independent Study/HomeStudy for grades K - 12
A Free public charter school, opened in 2011.
We have a focus on Leadership - incorporating the 
7 Habits of Highly Effective People
 - into everything we do - making it a ubiquitous approach to education. We also focus on the visual and performing arts, with the following being offered to our students: Spanish, Dance, Art, Video Production, Drama and much more!
Fully Accredited by the Schools Commission of the Western Association of Schools and Colleges.
Our Mission
<img src="https://static1.squarespace.com/static/53963f3ee4b0deed4a27f050/t/5510a81ae4b0dd8c456bad85/1427154972155/"  />
The Mission of Valley Life Charter Schools is to provide authentic opportunities for students to apply the skill sets for making good decisions. We focus on the whole student, and provide a well rounded education in the areas of academics, as well as visual and performing arts.
