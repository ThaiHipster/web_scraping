GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. (WOOD) The countdown continues for students all across West Michigan who are getting ready to head back to school. Brennecke Hinojosa, the principal of River City Scholars Charter Academy, operated by National Heritage Academies, has some tips on back to school safety and getting your kids back on a school schedule. Check out her tips here:
