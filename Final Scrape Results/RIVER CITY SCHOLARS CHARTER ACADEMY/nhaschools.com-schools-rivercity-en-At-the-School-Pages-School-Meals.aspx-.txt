School Meals		
Meal Programs
Healthy, balanced meals are available to all students.
Breakfast & Lunch
We participate in the 
Community Eligibility Provision​
 (CEP) program, which allows us to provide breakfast and lunch to all students at no charge. The CEP was established by the Department of Agriculture to ensure children are well prepared for a full day of school. Please see your school office for more information on participating in this program.
Parents please complete the Household Information Survey below and return to the school.
Household Information Survey
Breakfast
We offer breakfast each Monday between 8:45 a.m. and 9:00 a.m., and Tuesday-Friday from 7:45 a.m. to 8:00 a.m. 
Lunch
We offer hot lunch each full day of school.
