Heart of the Arts
Saturday, February 18, 2017
 JX Event Venue
 123 2nd St. N., Stillwater
If you have trouble watching this video, 
please click here
.
Heart of the Arts is the annual fundraiser for the Fine Arts Department.  The evening is a lively event with savory hors d’oeuvres, delicious sweets, fine wines and beer, live entertainment, raffles, a silent and live auctions and fund-a-need appeal.
Tickets
$45 – Single ticket includes food and two drink tickets.
$800 – The Hearts, An exclusive package for 10 guests.  Package includes10 tickets to the event, premier seating, exclusive individualized dessert by Joseph’s Restaurant,  a hand painted ceramic dessert plate created by the St. Croix Prep fine arts department faculty and a complimentary bottle of champagne provided by ONEHOPE.
Evening Entertainment
Bid on an array of unique auction item**, play the 50/50 raffle** and wine pull** while enjoying savory hors’ doeuvres , wine tasting provided by ONEHOPE, craft beer and fine wines.
Menu
The menu includes grilled chicken satays, chilled vegetarian spring rolls, chicken pot stickers with ginger soy sauce, crostini with toppings, fresh fruit platters and an assorted desserts.  Cash bar available for wine and beer.
Fund-A-Need Appeal
A special appeal will be made to specifically fund an expansion to the back of the stage of the Performing Arts Center which will include storage and set-building space.
