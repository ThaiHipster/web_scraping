General Information
For the second year, St. Croix Prep will be preparing food on-site.  With our new equipment, students will notice improved quality and expanded variety in hot-food menu offerings, while finding the same great variety and quality at our all-you-can-eat fresh fruit and salad bar.
St. Croix Prep is now using, an online menu resource called Nutrislice. Families can easily view information about what is on our school menu each day including images, nutrition and allergen information. This innovative and interactive site and mobile app will make our school menus more convenient and informative than ever before! Click here to 
visit the site or download the mobile app
, Nutrislice, through your mobile device app store.  Pick St. Croix Prep as the district (not Stillwater) to view the menus via the app.
