Employment Opportunities
St. Croix Preparatory Academy seeks professional educators who are passionate about building a school with a focus on academics, character, and leadership.  Successful candidates must also be committed to the classical model of education, be enthusiastic learners and leaders, as well as outstanding classroom teachers.
We are most interested in teachers who have an attitude that corresponds to the flexible and fast growing environment of St. Croix Prep.
St. Croix Prep offers staff growth opportunities, a positive work culture, and competitive compensation and benefits.  To view SCPA’s HITA policy, click
