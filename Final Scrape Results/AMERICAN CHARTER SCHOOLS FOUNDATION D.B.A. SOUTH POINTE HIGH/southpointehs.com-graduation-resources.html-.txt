2017 Graduation Events
SPECIAL NEWS FLASH!! GRADUATES NEED TO KNOW THIS!! You need a cap and gown to walk through the graduation line at graduation.  You need order your cap and gown immediately with Melissa Bernal in the front office.  The price for the cap gown is $43.21.
Senior Cap and Gown picture proofs may be picked up in the front office.
The Class of 2017 group picture, in your caps and gowns, will be on May 22, 2017 at 8:00 on the field next to North Campus.  
The Senior Breakfast with Mr. McGill will also be on May 22, 2017.  The Breakfast will at 10:00 a.m. and transportation from the school will be provided.  If you choose provide your own transportation, it will be located at the JB's Family Restaurant at 
3567 W Northern Ave, Phoenix, AZ 85051.
Graduation for the Class of 2017 is at the Orpheum Theatre on May 23, 2017 at 3:00 p.m at the Orpheum Theater, 203 W Adams Street, Phoenix, 85003.  Seating in the theater is limited; you will be allowed 10 tickets per graduate.  Handicap and wheelchair seating is available.  All graduates must attend rehearsal, which will take place on May 23rd at 9:30 a.m.
Your attire under your cap and gown should be professional.  Please do not wear sneakers, flip flops, jeans, shorts, or stiletto heels.  Gentlemen please wear dress slacks or khakis, a dress shirt or polo shirt with a collar, and dress shoes or casual dress shoes.  Ladies please wear dressy slacks, a skirt or dress with flats, dress shoes or pumps/heels.  If you have doubts about what to wear for graduation, please contact Melissa Bernal or Ms. Chavez.  Caps and gowns may not be altered.
