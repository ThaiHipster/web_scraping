A Quality School
South Pointe High School is accredited by the AdvancED, an independent organization that reviews public schools and colleges. The accreditation process consists of a thorough review, evaluation and graded report of the school's mission, performance goals, courses, teacher instruction, administrative procedures and achievement data, to name a few areas. To students and parents, accreditation means that student transcripts and grades are accepted by colleges throughout the country. 
South Pointe High School is a quality school, because we are accredited!
