Resources
As a charter public school, we are pleased to offer you a free and quality educational choice. This page will provide information concerning the policies, practices and procedures that are in place. School policies, code of conduct, daily schedule, attendance information and more can be found here.
In order for students to have academic success, it is essential that the school and home work together to achieve excellence. Each family is encouraged to be actively involved in the education process, so that we can continue to build a solid learning community: one with a strong academic foundation, within a safe, caring and respectful environment. 
We are pleased you have chosen South Pointe High School. We look forward to working with you to achieve educational excellence.
