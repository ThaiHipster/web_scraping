Mike Acquisto, Baseball Coach
Mike Acquisto has coached the SPHS baseball team for several years. He has been named Coach of the Year and has had winning teams. Coach Acquisto has also taught various subjects in past years, helping many classrooms around South Pointe High School and has earned a history degree from Arizona State University. We look forward to seeing a great team under his leadership during the 2018 season.
