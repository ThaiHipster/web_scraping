STEM Academy
Mission
Student success is the mission of the Cincinnati State STEM Academy ,focused on student centered learning by providing an academically rigorous curriculum built on a strong foundation in the sciences, technologies, engineering, and mathematics. We will provide experiences that motivate and cultivate the students for future educational and career success. 
Vision
Cincinnati State STEM Academy will provide an  academically rigorous core curricula focusing on science, technology,  engineering and math relevant to real-world experiences in order to prepare our  students to be life-long learners and productive members of society.
