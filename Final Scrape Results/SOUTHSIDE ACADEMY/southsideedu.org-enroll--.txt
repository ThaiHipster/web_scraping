Enrollment Process
For families interested in enrolling, please call our main office at  330-774-5562 
 to schedule a tour of the building, as well as to pick up an enrollment packet. Enrolling at South Side Academy is a very easy process, but seats fill up fast, so please contact us to schedule a tour today! Our office manager can also help make the process easier – please call for details. Or, complete the online enrollment form to the right to be contacted by school staff.
Enrollment Events
Please stay tuned for upcoming enrollment events.
Transportation
Parents interested in yellow bus transportation should complete the transportation form located in our enrollment packet. This information is submitted to the Youngstown bus garage and parents are then notified in August as to their child’s stop location as well as pick-up and drop-off times.
