Registration and Enrollment
Enrolling at Southwest Preparatory is fast and easy. 
Click on the application button below to get started. 
For  more information or assistance with registration, call 
210-302-0504
. 
Southwest 
Preparatory offers Pre-Kindergarten through 12th grade.
Our newest campus, Seguin Elementary is located at 2400 East Walnut and offers Pre-K through 6th grade.
​
The Northeast and New Directions Campuses located at 1258 Austin Hwy offer PK through 12th grade.
The Southeast Campus located at 735 SWW White Rd offers Pre-K through 12th grade.
​
The Northwest Elementary Campus located at 
41
51 Culebra Road offers Pre-K  through grade 6.
The Northwest Campus located at 6535 Culebra Road offers 7th-12th grade.  
*
Pre-Kinder Program
​
To enroll in our Pre-K Program please complete our enrollment application.  You will need to upload all listed required documentation and are required to prove eligibility 
(see link below for a list of criteria, you must meet one). Southwest Preparatory offers a Pre-Kindergarten 4 Program only.  For students who do not qualify, the tuition rate for Pre-Kinder students is $260.00 per month with the exception of August, June, and December which are calculated on a daily rate ($260.00/# days in the month).  Southwest Preparatory offer a 5% Military discount and a 20% Employee Discount.
PK Criteria II Table
Required Documentation for Enrollment
​
Proof of Residency (Utility Bill, Telephone, or Property Tax Bills; rental lease agreement)
​
Copy of Social Security Card (This disclosure is voluntary and the SS# is used for student ID in PEIMS)
​
Copy of proof of identity (birth record, driver's license, passport, military id, transcripts, report card, adoption record)
​
Transcripts (official or unofficial), report card, testing records, CSR, State Assessment scores, PGP, Career Pathways Portfolio, & Special Education records if applicable 
​
Shot Records indicating current immunizations
​
Proof of Guardianship, if applicable
