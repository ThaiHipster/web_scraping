Health Co-op
Welcome to the San Antonio School Health Co-op.  
The SA School Health Co-op provides services to many Charter Schools in San Antonio and the surrounding areas. 
Member Benefits
Free consultation regarding School Health Requirements as stated in The Texas Administrative Code, The Texas Department of State Health Services, and The Texas Education Agency.
Needs assessment performed for the individual School/School District and recommendations provided.
Members select services “a la carte” as needed to supplement their existing health services.
The cost savings can be significant as the cost decreases based on the number of services provided.
For more information, please call (210) 302-0505.
