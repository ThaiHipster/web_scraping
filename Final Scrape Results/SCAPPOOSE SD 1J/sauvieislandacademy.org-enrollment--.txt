Applications Accepted Daily for Current Openings
Enrollment applications for current openings are accepted daily. You can fill out an 
application online or download a printable version here
. We currently have openings in grades 5th-8th.
Enrollment Dates for Wait-listed Classes and 2018-2019 Academic Year
Open enrollment for the 2018-2019 Academic year begins on 02/01/18.
Applications are accepted • February 1, 2018
Open Enrollment Closes • March 23, 2018 
Applications accepted after this date will NOT be included in the lottery and will be added to the waitlist after the lottery in the order in which they were received.
Lottery  • April 18, 2018, 5:30pm
The lottery is open to the public. Parents and students are not required to attend in order to secure their student’s place in the class they applied for or position on the waiting list.
