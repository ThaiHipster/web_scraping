Technology at WPACES
West Philadelphia Achievement Charter Elementary School  views technology as a relevant tool for learning as well as a medium for communication, inquiry and innovation in the  21st century.  WPACES strives to enhance student learning through technology and foster an academic environment and school culture that has embedded appropriate technology to support student learning across all curricular areas.
WPACES has integrated a variety of relevant technology tools  to support and facilitate 21st century teaching and learning. These learning tools include hardware and software for student assessment, evaluation, collaborative learning, research, enrichment and remediation while remaining fully 
CIPA
 and 
COPPA
 compliant. 
Available Technology Resources Include: 
PC Workstations in each classroom
Flatscreen TVs in each classroom
3 Mobile iPad Learning Carts
75 iPads
4 Google Chromebook Carts
100 Chromebooks
2 Computer Labs
Interactive Boards
Digital Signage Panels
Multimedia Carts (LCD Projectors & Speakers)
HighSpeed Internet
Wireless Connectivity 
School-wide Digital Surveillance
