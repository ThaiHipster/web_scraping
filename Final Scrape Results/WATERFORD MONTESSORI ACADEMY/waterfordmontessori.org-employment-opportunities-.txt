Employment Opportunities
Classroom Assistants
Waterford Montessori Academy, a public charter school in Waterford, Michigan, is accepting applications for part-time classroom assistant positions in our early childhood, lower elementary, and upper elementary classrooms, for the 2016-17 school year (mid-August through mid-June).
We are looking for candidates with the following characteristics:
- Must love children, be kind and considerate
- Must be a team player – you may be asked to help in any area of the school
- Applicants with Montessori experience are preferred, however, we will train the right candidates
Applicants who possess at least 90 hours of college credit and eligible for a substitute permit are preferred, but it is not required.  
Please send a cover letter and resume to 
info@waterfordmontessori.org
