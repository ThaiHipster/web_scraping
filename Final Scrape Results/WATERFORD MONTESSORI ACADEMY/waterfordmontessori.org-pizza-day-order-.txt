Pizza Day Order
Lunch is served with one piece of pizza, one fruit, one veggie and small bottled water. 
Pizza Day Order Forms
12/15/17 Pizza Day Order Form
Full Year Pizza Day Order Form 12/15/17
Orders are due on the Tuesday before pizza day 
by 3:30 p.m
. Because we must order the pizza in advance, we 
cannot
 accept late orders.
Pizza orders are now available online using PayPal. The order forms are available on the right by pizza day. 
To place an order:
    1.    Click
 the order form on the right.. (Be sure you are selecting the correct date)
    2.    Choose the meal selection.
    3.    Select your child's teacher. (There are two drop down menus. One for preschool - 3rd grade and one for 4th-7th grade)
    4.    Enter your child's first and last name.
    5.    Click the Add to Cart button and then you can proceed to the checkout. 
To purchase additional slice of pizza: 
    1.    Click Continue Shopping.
    2.    Select the extra slice.
    3.    Select your child's teacher.
    4.    Enter your child's first and last name.
    5.    Click the Add to Cart button.
To purchase multiple pizza days:
    1.    Click Continue Shopping.
    2.    Go back to the Pizza Day Order webpage.
    3.    Select the order form by date and repeat steps 2-5 in placing your order.
(Your child's teacher and your child's full name must be completed for each item you add to your cart)
