About the Management Company
American Montessori Solutions, Inc. (AMS) core staff has extensive and varied experience in the Montessori educational field, with a decades-long history of working with, administering, consulting with, and managing private, public and charter school programs in the state of Michigan.
AMS staff members have channeled their energy, resources and expertise into providing solutions for excellence in education, and into solving education inequity. Their on-site presence at WMA will provide support, evaluation and direction to teachers and classroom assistants, and will assess the daily progress of the students. Throughout the school-year, AMS will facilitate professional development in-services for staff, as well as parent education seminars.
