Curriculum
The Summit High School Curriculum
 Summit High School has designed a student curriculum that is based on  the Arizona State Standards. Objectives are taught using a variety of  learning activities and addressing multiple learning modalities.  Reading, writing, and technology skills are integrated throughout the  curriculum and are an integral part of all content area classes.  Textbooks and all other instructional materials are selected on the  basis of their alignment with the Arizona State Standards.
