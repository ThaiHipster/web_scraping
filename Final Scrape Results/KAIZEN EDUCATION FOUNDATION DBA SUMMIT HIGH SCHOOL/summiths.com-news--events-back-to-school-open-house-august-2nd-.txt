Back to School Open House - August 2nd
		7/29/2017				
		0 Comments	
Summit High School will be hosting an Open House for all current, newly enrolled and those interested in enrolling at Summit High School. The Open House will take place on Wednesday, August 2nd from 12:00 pm to 4:00 pm. Current and newly enrolled students can pick up their schedule, take their ID picture and get a brand new Summit t-shirt for the 2017/18 school year. The office will be enrolling students during Open House. This year's Open House will have a boardwalk theme with games, food, and prizes.​
