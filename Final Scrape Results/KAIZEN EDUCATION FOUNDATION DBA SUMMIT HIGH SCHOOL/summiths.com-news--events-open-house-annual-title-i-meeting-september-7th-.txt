Open House - Annual Title I Meeting - September 7th
		8/28/2017				
		0 Comments	
Open House will take place on Thursday, September 7th from 5:00 pm to 7:00 pm. This an excellent opportunity for parents and guardians to meet their student's teachers as well as Summit High School Staff. Representatives from area community colleges and community resources will be in attendance to meet with students and families. Summit's annual Title I meeting will also take place during Open House. Food and raffle prizes will also be available while supplies last. For more information regarding Open House or the Title I meeting, please contact Summit High School at (602) 258-8959.
