Quality Education
Summit High School  offers a quality curriculum fully aligned with Arizona  state standards. In addition, we recognize the value of a well-rounded  learning experience. Therefore, we offer many opportunities and elective  courses for a variety of interests and needs. 
 Our caring  teaching staff consists of highly qualified teachers who  work hard every day to ensure that each student receives the finest education  possible. Our small campus allows our teaching staff to  personalize the learning experience for every student.
