TICKETS ON SALE NOW!
TAPA’s production of Benches: A Night of One Act Plays is now on sale! Tickets are $10 in advance and there are only 40 seats available for each show, so tickets will sell out fast. Come see these powerful and thought-provoking plays on December 14th & 15th. The shows start promptly at 7:00pm.
Don't wait to buy tickets, or you may miss out. No tickets will be sold at the door.
Contact Mr. White, Theater Artist in Residence, for ticket purchase and production information: 
mr.white@tapaprovidence.org
, (401) 432-7881.

TAPA’s 8th Grade Semi – Formal
Dinner Dance and Awards Night 2016
Come celebrate the end of the 8th grade and transition to 9th grade with a special awards night, Chinese buffet and dancing!
Where: Lemongrass, 1138 Post Rd, Warwick
When: Friday, June 24th 2016
Drop off: 6:00pm – 6:30pm
Pick up: 9:30pm – 10:00pm
