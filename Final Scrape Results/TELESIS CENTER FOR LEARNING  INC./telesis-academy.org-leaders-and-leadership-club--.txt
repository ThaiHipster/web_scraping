Tammy Breece
			2014-04-07T09:56:56+00:00																
Leader’s and Leadership Club is a club that practices the art of leadership asking guest speakers to come to club meetings to share their tips on good leadership performance. Then club members go out and put it into action with various events and participation in community activities.
